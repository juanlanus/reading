Usability Test Feedback

The Books Smart Reader

This is the most relevant part of the usability test: when you give us your feedback.

There are questions with a closed set of answers, that you answer by clicking a choice, and open answers (optional) where you can write whatever you feel it fits.

Written answers can be in English or Spanish, it' your choice.

Multiple choice answers are such that "1" (the leftmost choice) means "worst" and "5" (the rightmost choice) means "best", and "2", "3" and "4" mean intermediate satisfaction levels as usual. In addition to the choices, some of these questions provide a space where you can optionally explain yourself in written.
