Usability Test Consent

The Books Smart Reader

Protocol Director: Juan Lanus

DESCRIPTION: You are invited to participate in a research study on the “Books Smart Reader” application, a software to aid those who read lengthy texts on screen to read more with less strain and more comfort.

You’ll be asked to read a text and give us qualitative feedback, not on the text content but the way it is presented to you and the features to manipulate it.

Your actions you take to operate the Book Smart Reader user interface will be recorded, and also

TIME INVOLVEMENT: Your participation will take approximately 20 to 50 minutes.

RISKS AND BENEFITS: There are no risks associated with this study. There are also no benefits, besides helping make the world a better place.

PAYMENTS: You will no payment for your participation.

YOUR RIGHTS: If you have read this form and have decided to participate in this project, please understand your participation is voluntary and you have the right to withdraw your consent or discontinue participation at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. The alternative is not to participate. You have the right to refuse to answer particular questions.

About your individual privacy, we will publish data resulting from the study and you will be mentioned by your first name. Your personal information and contact data will be held private.
